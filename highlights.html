üì∏ The School Project

Nathan Wasolo

In September, Nathan and Carol visited the school as part of the group's committment to regular check-ins with the school administration.

In a meeting with the headmistress and her Deputy, it was agreed that in November, they will return to donate books for the upper classes as per a list of titles compiled by the Deputy Headmaster. They will also participate in a tree planting excercise, a total of 150 trees will be planted, in an area that was jointly inspected and approved with the administration.

In typical class of '80-86 fashion, many of you are already contributing to these causes and we appreciate your generosity.

The school leadership also highlighted pressing infrastructure needs like the need for water tanks (to support both the school and the tree project), and the need for a boundary wall to protect the school land from encroachment.

We shall be calling on some in the group to champion these infrastructure projects by scoping the work, costing options and directing the group on the way forward.
